OneOffForged is a dedicated forged wheel manufacturer based in China. With a full range of advanced CNC machines and decades of experience, we deliver fully customizable forged wheels tailored to each client’s needs. Whether it’s an aggressive street setup or a show-ready design, we support all custom specs, finishes, and styles.

Backed by direct factory support and no middlemen, we ensure consistent quality, fast production timelines, and personalized service. From engineering to packaging, every set is made with precision and passion.
