What’s the typical production time?

Most orders take about 25 days for production. Shipping usually adds another 15–20 days depending on your location.

Is a deposit required?

Yes, we require a 50% down payment to start production. The balance and shipping cost are due before delivery.

Can I customize my wheels?

All wheels are custom made-to-order. You can specify size, offset, bolt pattern, finish, and design features.

Do you accept international orders?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Our team can help calculate shipping costs and timelines for your region.

How do I place an order?

Contact us via Instagram or email with your vehicle info and preferred style. We’ll walk you through the rest of the process.
